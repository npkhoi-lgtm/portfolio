About Me

A seasoned professional with over 25 years of progressive experience in senior factory management roles, including Operations, Production, and R&D. My expertise spans various industries such as Tissue & Carton Paper, Jewelry, and Indoor/Outdoor Furniture manufacturing.

I specialize in technical operations, R&D, supply chain management, production planning, factory facility operations, and project management. I am proficient in implementing Industry 4.0 concepts, digital transformation (ERP/MES/Automation), and setting up greenfield factories.

My strategic approach involves utilizing SWOT analysis to set SMART goals, developing OGSM, BSC, OPEX, and AOP budgets to align manufacturing and quality activities with business roadmaps, and ensuring clear KPI implementation for cost control and efficiency. I also have extensive experience with Quality Management Systems (QMS), CSR, BSCI, SMETA, and ESG audits.
